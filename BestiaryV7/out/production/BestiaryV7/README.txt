
    Bestiary
----------------

Inspired by the fantastic souls games and dungeon crawlers of this generation,
Bestiary is a text-based adventure role-playing game that allows users to take control of a
traveler arriving at the town of Rutherford in the outskirts of the mythical kingdom of Bestiar.
The player can explore the region, fight supernatural beasts, fish, and catch insects at his/her leisure,
providing for a unique modern fantasy take on the genre of text-based games.
